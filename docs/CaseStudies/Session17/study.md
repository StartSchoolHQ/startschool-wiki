Lovable was launched in January 2024. In October 2024, it raised almost 7 million in pre-seed funding  
Bolt.new was launched in October 2024. In January 2025, it raised 105 million.  

Base44 was launched in January 2025. In June 2025, it was acquired by Wix for 80 million  

## The big question: How?

What made base44 attract thousands of users without a huge ad spend in a space with well-known and well-founded solutions?

## First part: 30 minutes

Spend 5 minutes inputting the same product prompt into base 44 and on bolt.new / lovable. What difference do you observe between these services? Discuss in your group  

Study the ProductHunt page of Base 44. What was their feature roadmap and what can you learn from it when it comes to your own product?  

How was the product marketed? Study the reddit thread by the founder, as well as his Linkedin / other socials  

Prepare to share the key learnings in a joined discussion
